About Us

History of JustStay

JustStay began its journey in the heart of Ahmedabad with a simple mission — to offer a home-like stay with world-class hospitality. From a small boutique property, it has grown into a trusted name for both business and leisure travelers.
Life’s too short for boring stays — that’s why at JustStay, we blend comfort with charm, minus the awkward hotel slippers. Sure, we can’t fix Monday blues, but we can promise a bed so cozy, you’ll forget it's even Monday.

Why Choose us?

At JustStay Hotel, we don’t just offer rooms — we offer comfort, care, and a little bit of magic in every stay. Located in the heart of Ahmedabad, we blend modern luxury with a warm, homely vibe that guests love.
Unmatched comfort meets timeless elegance — because you deserve more than just a stay. ✔️ From spotless rooms to 24/7 support, we make every detail count for your perfect getaway.
